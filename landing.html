F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • For how long are my records kept?

    For as long as computers are still on the earth. When you open up an account with us, you are allocated online storage that is only managed by you.

  • Does Chocolate Cosmos have User support?

    We have a team that is always online to help users with any issues. Chat with us online through a button on the right bottom of your screen.

  • How long does the license last

    The license covers a period of 6 months and after this the user will be required to renew their licencse.

  • Is my data lost when the license expires?

    No user data is lost or deleted after license expiry and this data is available whenever the user renews their license.

  • How much storage am I allocated?

    Every user is allocated upto 10GB online storage on our servers. Considering that you are storing mainly text, this takes a very long time to fill up and even then, more space will be allocated.
